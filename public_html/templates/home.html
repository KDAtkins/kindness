Welcome to KindHub! Sign up to create a hub where anyone can easily donate to or find the help they need. As your hub gets better you will start to earn reputation and levels. If you are trying to donate or get the supplies you need, these will be listed in the section called "Find a Hub." It's just that easy.

FAQ

  • Q: What is KindHub?
  • A: KindHub is an app designed to streamline the donation process for those on the giving and receiving end of the process. Fudnamentally, KindHub gives a platform for anyone who wants to set up a donation center for their community.
  • Q: Who are the developers?
  • A: Jermaine Jennings, Calder Benjamin, Marcus Caldeira, Nick Pascetti and Michael Romero.
